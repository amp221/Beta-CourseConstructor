Lab Sciences

There are a few lab sciences that are favored by CS majors. If you tend to like math Physics might be a great option. Students who are interested in the natural world find that life science and exploring the universe. Chemistry is great for those that are interested in both math and natural science.

Math

Some electives count towards other cs pathways. The Data Science Pathway requires Calculus 3 and Linear Algebra. If you like Discrete Math, Linear Algebra might be a good choice for a technical elective.

Ethics

There are quite a few ethics classes offered but not all of them fill the CS ethics requirement. Intro to Ethics and Engineering and Ethics both meet the requirement and are offered on North Campus each semester. If you find another option that you are interested in check with your advisor to make sure it will count towards your degree.

Project Sequence

The CS project sequence gives you a chance to explore in depth a specific area of computer science. This requirement is generally completed over one or two semesters and in which you learn about the subject and complete a project. Some popular choices are Database 1 & 2, GUI 1 & 2, AI and Machine Learning. Be aware that not all of these classes are offered each semester.
